Our Mission

Dalaw Kalinga is a sustenance program dedicated to providing essential resources and support to individuals and families in need. Our mission is to create positive change in our community by offering food, healthcare, education, and other vital services. We strive to build a world where everyone has the chance to thrive, fostering a sense of hope and empowerment among those we serve.

Our Values

  • Compassion for those in need: We believe in the power of empathy and kindness. When someone is struggling, we come together to offer support and understanding. It's about being there for each other, lifting each other up, and making a positive difference in the lives of those who need it most.
  • Transparency and accountability: We value honesty and openness in everything we do. We believe that being transparent about our actions and decisions builds trust and integrity. Accountability means taking responsibility for our actions and their impacts, ensuring that we are always striving to do the right thing.
  • Commitment to community development: We are dedicated to the growth and well-being of our community. This means actively participating in initiatives that promote social, economic, and environmental progress. We believe in working together to create a better future for everyone, fostering a sense of collective responsibility and growth.
  • Respect for diversity and inclusion: We celebrate the differences that make each of us unique. We believe in creating an environment where everyone feels welcomed, respected, and valued. By embracing diversity and promoting inclusion, we ensure that everyone has equal opportunities to contribute and succeed.
